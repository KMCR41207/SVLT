About FleetFlow

What Our Platform Enables

FleetFlow is designed to connect every stakeholder in the logistics chain - from the driver on the road to the owner in the office.

  • The truck drivers can login to their Mobile App.
  • The truck drivers can update data such as distance travelled by the truck as per odometer, expenses such as fuel expenses, tolls, etc. incurred during their trips.
  • The truck owners can login to their Mobile App.
  • The truck owners can view reports regarding the truck such as usage of tyres, change of tyres, insurance coverage, and number of kilometers the truck has travelled, truck drivers information, and so on.
  • The truck owners can view the expenses incurred for the truck and its trip, such as fuel expenses, tolls, etc.
  • The Admin can login to the Web App.
  • The truck owners can update the data regarding the truck such as usage of tyres, change of tyres, insurance coverage, and number of kilometers the truck has travelled, and so on. The truck owners can also upload and manage the data of truck drivers.

What We Offer

Powerful Tools for Every Role

Each user type gets a dedicated experience with tools built exactly for their needs.

Truck Drivers

Mobile-first driver tools
  • Login to the dedicated Driver Mobile App.
  • Update distance travelled via odometer readings.
  • Log trip expenses - fuel costs, tolls & more.
  • Receive real-time job assignments from fleet owners.
  • View trip history and personal earnings summary.
  • Get notified on schedule changes and delivery updates.

Fleet Owners

Full fleet visibility & control
  • Login to the fleet owner web dashboard.
  • View tyre usage, insurance coverage & km reports.
  • Monitor fuel expenses, tolls and all trip costs.
  • Upload & manage truck details and driver data.
  • Track revenue, profit & costs via analytics graphs.
  • Assign drivers to trucks and manage active trips.
  • Monitor truck condition - tyres, fuel & service records.

Shippers

Post loads & track deliveries
  • Post loads with pickup & delivery details.
  • Get instantly matched with available trucks.
  • Track your shipment status in real-time.
  • Secure payments & digital invoicing.

Complete Visibility of Your Fleet's Health

FleetFlow goes beyond simple tracking. Get deep insights into every aspect of truck condition so you never miss a service, replacement, or expiry.

  • Individual tyre health monitoring for all 6 tyres per truck
  • Fuel level tracking with colour-coded low fuel alerts
  • Last service date, days since service & overdue warnings
  • Insurance coverage & document expiry reminders
  • Odometer-based distance tracking per trip
  • Revenue, cost & profit graphs per truck and fleet-wide
