How It Works

Get up and running in minutes with our streamlined onboarding process.

  1. 1

    Register & Set Up Your Profile

    Create your account, choose your role, and complete your business profile to get started on the platform.

  2. 2

    Post or Browse Available Loads

    Shippers post freight requirements; drivers and fleet owners browse and discover loads that match their routes and capacity.

  3. 3

    Bid, Accept & Assign Drivers

    Submit competitive bids, accept the best offer, and assign the right driver to the job — all within the platform.

  4. 4

    Track Shipment in Real-Time

    Monitor every trip with live GPS tracking, status updates, and instant notifications for all stakeholders.

  5. 5

    Confirm Delivery & Process Payment

    Digitally confirm delivery, auto-generate invoices, and process secure payments — no paperwork required.
